Simplex 1910

The Simplex 1920 is one of the last 3 simplex cars remaining.It was created in 1920 and certified for road use in 1925

It was originally owned by the 7th Earl of Fitzwilliam. His Coat of Arms is visible on the side doors.

It was then purchased by Sir Norman Adsetts who donated it to the Kelham Island Museum in 1998
